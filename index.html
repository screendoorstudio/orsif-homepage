Two Threats. One Mission.

ORSIF is the only organization addressing BOTH occupational hazards facing fluoroscopy lab personnel.

Radiation Exposure

Interventional cardiologists face 10x higher radiation exposure than diagnostic radiologists.

  • Cancer and brain tumors
  • Cataracts and eye damage
  • Thyroid disease
  • Cardiovascular effects

Musculoskeletal Injuries

Lead aprons weighing 10-15 pounds are worn for hours, causing chronic injuries.

  • Lumbar spine problems
  • Cervical disc disease
  • Chronic back pain
  • Career-limiting injuries

The hidden cost: $75-100 million annually in healthcare worker injuries
